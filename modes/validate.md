# Mode: validate

## Goal

Validate one market hypothesis and produce a decision-ready report.

## Steps

1. Clarify the hypothesis into customer + pain + wedge.
2. Gather external evidence (competitors, pricing, reviews/community, demand signals).
3. Evaluate evidence quality (snippet-only vs fetched-page support).
4. Score viability dimensions and confidence.
5. Return: verdict, score, risks, unknowns, and next experiments.

## Required Sections in Report

- Market summary
- Source coverage
- Competitor and pricing observations
- Risks and unknowns
- Next validation experiments
